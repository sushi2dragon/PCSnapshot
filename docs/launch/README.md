# PC Snapshot — Launch & Growth Guide

*Produced 2026-07-16 from the repository itself plus cited market research. Assumes the Windows v1 is production-ready; covers everything else required to turn it into a commercially successful product. Solo founder in India, no funding, minimal budget.*

## The strategy in one paragraph

PC Snapshot launches as a **"workspace snapshot tool"** — a category it defines rather than a crowded one it enters — aimed first at **Windows developers and technical freelancers**, the segment for whom every differentiator (exact tab restore, terminal directories, VS Code workspaces, multi-monitor layout) fires at once. It sells **direct** via a static site and **Paddle** (merchant of record — the pragmatic path for an Indian founder, since Stripe is invite-only for Indian entities), at **$29 one-time with 1 year of updates** (₹1,499 India via regional pricing), behind a **30-day full-featured no-card trial** and a permanent **Free tier** (full fidelity, 3 most recent snapshots) that powers word of mouth. The marketing plan spends no money: Hacker News, Product Hunt, and Reddit for the launch spike; SEO comparison content, build-in-public X, and creator outreach for the compounding tail — all anchored on the two proven pillars the research surfaced: **local-first privacy as an acquisition message** (Obsidian, LocalSend) and **never changing the deal on existing customers** (the Stardock Fences cautionary tale). Fixed costs run ~$25-40/month (~₹2,200-3,500), dominated by the code-signing certificate, so the business is profitable almost immediately and is built to compound through trust, owned channels, and visible shipping rather than launch-week spikes.

## Locked decisions (consistent across all documents)

Free tier: full fidelity, 3 most recent snapshots, forever · Pro: **$29 one-time** (launch week $24), 1 year updates, never stops working, 40%-off renewal, 3 activations · Trial: 30 days, no card · Refunds: 14 days unconditional · Stack: Paddle + Keygen + SSL.com OV + Tauri NSIS/updater + GitHub Releases + Astro/Cloudflare + Zoho Desk + MailerLite/Resend + Sentry (opt-in dialog only) · Telemetry: none · Launch: GitHub beta (source-available) August 2026, then direct + winget paid launch **2026-10-07** via HN + Product Hunt + Reddit (revised schedule in doc 08; the original full-time Aug 5 plan in doc 04 is superseded).

## Where professional advice is required (not optional)

A chartered accountant for GST/LUT/export-income and payout treatment; a lawyer for a one-time EULA/privacy/terms review and a trademark opinion on the brand name. Budget ₹35,000-90,000 (~$400-1,035). Nothing in these documents is legal or tax advice.
